Bottled Ocean - Focus Timer for Reading

About Bottled Ocean

Bottled Ocean is a minimalist focus timer designed specifically for readers and learners. Inspired by the calm ocean, it helps you create a distraction-free reading space.

How to Use

  • Open the app and set your desired focus time
  • Tap the timer to start your reading session
  • Enjoy peaceful, focused reading time
  • The timer will notify you when your session is complete

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I customize the timer duration?
A: You can adjust the timer duration directly in the app interface. Simply tap on the time display to set your preferred reading session length.
Q: Does Bottled Ocean work offline?
A: Yes! Bottled Ocean works completely offline. No internet connection is required to use the timer.
Q: Is my reading data saved?
A: All your timer settings and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store any of your data on external servers.
Q: Can I use Bottled Ocean for studying or other activities?
A: Absolutely! While designed for reading, Bottled Ocean is perfect for any focused activity including studying, writing, or meditation.

常见问题

问：如何自定义计时器时长？
答：您可以直接在应用界面中调整计时器时长。只需点击时间显示区域，即可设置您喜欢的阅读时长。
问：Bottled Ocean 可以离线使用吗？
答：可以！Bottled Ocean 完全支持离线使用。使用计时器不需要网络连接。
问：我的阅读数据会被保存吗？
答：您的所有计时器设置和偏好都存储在您的设备本地。我们不会在外部服务器上收集或存储您的任何数据。
问：我可以用 Bottled Ocean 进行学习或其他活动吗？
答：当然可以！虽然专为阅读设计，但 Bottled Ocean 适用于任何需要专注的活动，包括学习、写作或冥想。
